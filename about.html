About Us

We are Marc and Alba,
an Irish and Spanish couple with a passion for traveling and exploring the world.
Having lived in various corners of the globe, we understand what it takes to create a remarkable and unforgettable stay.
We aim to provide you with a comfortable and extraordinary experience in one of the most beautiful places on Earth.
Our glamping site is sustainable because we love our planet and are committed to eco-friendly and sustainable tourism.

Location
Our glamping site is nestled in the mountains, just 2 km from the picturesque village of Frigiliana.
With stunning sea views, it's the perfect blend of tranquility and natural beauty.
The Mediterranean coast and the vibrant town of Nerja are just a 10-minute drive away,
making it easy to enjoy the beach and explore the local attractions.
